Our mission
Through our mission, OpenNom aims to empower individuals worldwide by bridging the skills gap, breaking down barriers to education, promoting environmental awareness,
and fostering inclusive and collaborative learning environments
Bridging the skills gap:
  • Empower individuals worldwide with essential skills in mathematics and literacy
  • Provide accessible, personalized, and engaging learning experiences to bridge the gap
Breaking Down Barriers to Education:
  • Create inclusive learning environments that empower all learners, regardless of their circumstances
Promoting Environmental Awareness:
  • Cultivate a generation that understands and acts upon pressing global environmental issues
Multilingual and Inclusive Education:
  • Provide multilingual support to cater to learners' diverse linguistic backgrounds.
  • Break language barriers and ensure equal access to knowledge.

Meet us
Our team, the Buryats, an indigenous group in Russia, brings a unique perspective shaped by personal experiences of discrimination,
cultural adaptation, and integration challenges.
Witnessing limited access to education in our native language, we created OpenNom AI to bridge educational gaps and foster integration.
With diverse backgrounds and shared experiences, we are dedicated to driving positive change and providing equitable educational opportunities globally, united in our mission for a more inclusive future
